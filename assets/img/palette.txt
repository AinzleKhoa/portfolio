Hex Code	Suggested Use	Why
#062c43	Navbar background, footer background, or main BG overlay	Deep navy – perfect for strong contrast and a sleek, professional base.
#054569	Buttons, active links, hover states	Adds depth and variation while staying dark. Great for interactivity.
#5591a9	Headings, icons, accent borders, or cards	Mid-tone blue – balances well between contrast and softness.
#9ccddc	Text highlights, section backgrounds, info boxes	Soft sky blue – calming and excellent for separating sections gently.
#ced7e0	Main body text, subtext, background for cards	Lightest – ideal for legible text on dark backgrounds or vice versa.
